About DFI

The official governing body for darts in India, promoting the sport through tournaments, training, and development programs.

Our Mission

The Darts Federation of India (DFI) was established to standardize and promote the sport across the country. We organize national championships, maintain player rankings, and provide training resources.

Our vision is to develop darts at grassroots levels while creating pathways for Indian players to compete internationally.

Darts Rules & Formats

Learn the official rules and popular game variations recognized by DFI.

Basic Rules

  • Standard board height: 1.73m (5'8") from floor to bullseye
  • Throwing distance: 2.37m (7'9.25") from board face
  • Players throw three darts per turn
  • Scoring: Singles (face value), Doubles (×2), Triples (×3)
  • Bullseye: Outer bull = 25, Inner bull = 50 points

Popular Formats

  • 501: Start at 501, subtract each turn's score, finish on a double
  • Cricket: Close numbers 15-20 and bull by hitting each three times
  • Around the Clock: Hit numbers 1-20 in sequence
  • Killer: Players "kill" numbers by hitting them three times
  • Shanghai: Score single, double and triple of each number
